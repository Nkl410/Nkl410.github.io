Inventory Digitization Platform

End-to-end management of a large-scale industrial inventory operation, from platform design and data automation to logistics coordination and revenue generation.

Context

As part of my role at CircularPlace, I led the digitization of a large industrial inventory during a full building clearance operation. The project required structuring thousands of heterogeneous objects, coordinating on-site logistics, and building a dedicated data platform to support reuse and resale.

Objectives

  • Digitize and structure a large-scale physical inventory
  • Automate product sheet creation for reuse marketplaces
  • Enable value estimation and faster resale
  • Coordinate operational and logistical workflows

My Role

I acted as both project lead and technical contributor, overseeing the entire lifecycle of the operation.

Project & Operations

  • Led and coordinated a large-scale inventory operation on site
  • Managed teams, workflows, and operational constraints
  • Designed inventory processes aligned with reuse objectives
  • Coordinated logistics from collection to resale channels

Technical & Data

  • Designed and developed an inventory digitization platform
  • Structured and cleaned large-scale inventory datasets
  • Automated product sheet generation
  • Integrated data enrichment and valuation logic

Platform & Architecture

Data Layer

Automation

  • Time-saving workflows for operators

AI Enrichment

  • Integrated into the inventory platform
